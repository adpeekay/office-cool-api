This tool estimates the annual cooling energy, cost and carbon impacts of different glazing technologies for office buildings around the world. Results are based on representative climate conditions for each country using typical values for electricty price (in USD) and grid carbon intensity (kgCO2/kWh). It also includes energy produced from the PV glass as a reduced energy cost.

Steps to success: Select a country and wait a monment - the map will center on that country - that's all! below the map a chart and a table will populate after a while

What am I looking at? Bar chart - each bar represents a glazing type and the amount energy (kWh) require to cool that office over a year Line graph - shows the cost of that cooling energy from local electricity prices Table - compares Polysolar's PV Glass with Solar Control glass against float glass on CO2 and energycost savings info on the local grid price and carbon intensity is also shown
